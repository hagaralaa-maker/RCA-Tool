Google Sheets Integration Setup

This dashboard is currently running on localized demo data. To connect this directly to your live Google Sheet and automate report generation, follow one of the methods below.

Method 1: API / Webhook (Recommended)

Use a tool like Zapier or Make to push new QA rows from Google Sheets directly to this dashboard's backend API.

POST https://api.yourcompany.com/qa/ingest
{
  "agent": "Agent Name",
  "queue": "Phone",
  "score": 85,
  "failedAttributes": ["Empathy"]
}

Method 2: CSV Export/Import

Export your Google Sheet as a CSV and upload it directly here to update your dashboard data manually.

Click to upload or drag and drop your Google Sheets CSV here.

Required columns: Date, Agent, Queue, Score, Failed Attributes
